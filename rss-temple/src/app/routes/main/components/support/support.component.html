Hotkeys

RSS Temple has several hotkeys to use on the "Feed" pages, to make navigation easier.

Key Action
J Select the next article
K Select the previous article
M Toggle to read/unread state of the currently-selected article
N Open the currently-selected article in a new tab
R Reload the current feed view
What is RSS/Atom?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) and Atom are standard "web feed" technologies. They are a way for computers to notify each other when new "articles" are posted.
